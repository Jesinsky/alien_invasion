Aliens Invasion

A game with a ship that is on the screen center.
The player can move the ship right and left and shooting bullets using keyboard.
When the game begins the sky fills with aliens and moves across the screen.
To destroy aliens the player must shoot them, when it kills one of them another appears and moces faster than the previous ones.
If any aliens hit the player he looses one of the ship counters.
Loosing 3 ships the game ends.
